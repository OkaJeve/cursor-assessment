POST 1 (Topic: The Product-Content Fit & ROI Attribution Fallacy):
[Hook from LinkedIn]: Most B2B companies are completely blind to their true content attribution. If you think a single blog post drove a closed-won deal, you're looking at a broken model.

[Core Strategy Text]:
- The Product-Content Fit Metric: Content should not be judged solely on organic keyword rankings or broad traffic spikes. True fit happens when the content answers a specific pain point that only your product’s unique feature set can solve.
- Linear vs. Multi-Touch Reality: Buyers ingest 10 to 15 different assets across LinkedIn, newsletters, and organic search before converting. Attribution software always defaults to assigning credit to the last touchpoint, leading companies to over-invest in basic bottom-of-funnel conversion pages while starving their core narrative pieces.
- Actionable Framework: Shift your evaluation metrics toward overall pipeline impact. Track how many closed-won accounts engaged with your primary narrative pieces during their buying journey, rather than tracking isolated, standalone landing page conversions.

POST 2 (Topic: Narrative-Driven Growth Over Brand-Less SEO):
[Hook from LinkedIn]: Commoditized AI content is a race to the bottom. If anyone can spin up a 2,000-word guide on a keyword in 30 seconds, your only real moat is having an actual point of view.

[Core Strategy Text]:
- The Brand Moat Strategy: In an era of unlimited, cheap content generation, classical programmatic SEO frameworks face declining returns. True search engine moats are built entirely on original research, strong opinions, and native storytelling.
- The Interview-First Architecture: Instead of letting an AI engine summarize the existing top 10 Google results, base every piece of content on a deep interview with an internal or external subject matter expert. 
- LLM Recognition Rules: AI models and conversational engines identify and surface unique brand perspectives, proprietary frameworks, and custom case studies because they cannot be replicated by basic predictive text algorithms.
